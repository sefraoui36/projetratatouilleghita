The Preparation of Pumpkin Soup:

In a cozy countryside kitchen, the aroma of autumn begins with a single ingredient: pumpkin. Sweet, earthy, and golden-orange — the soul of the season.

We begin by roasting peeled chunks of fresh pumpkin until they become soft and slightly caramelized. Their natural sweetness deepens in the heat, awakening memories of fall harvests and flickering candles.

In a warm pot, we sauté finely chopped onions and a hint of garlic in a drizzle of olive oil. Once translucent, the roasted pumpkin is folded in, followed by a fragrant mix of cinnamon, nutmeg, and a whisper of clove.

We then pour in a rich vegetable broth and let everything simmer gently — blending the ingredients into a melody of warmth and comfort.

Once tender and infused, the mixture is blended into a silky-smooth soup. To finish, we stir in a swirl of cream for that luxurious, velvety texture.

Each spoonful delivers a gentle heat, creamy comfort, and the delicate dance of autumn spices. Served with a sprinkle of roasted pumpkin seeds or a drizzle of herb oil, this soup becomes more than a meal — it’s an embrace in a bowl.

A celebration of the season. A moment of calm.

Recipe crafted with love and passion. Enjoy!
